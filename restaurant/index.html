Restaurant Description

Welcome to Taj Mahal Cafe, the best Indian restaurant in Austin. Here you can find cuisine from all over the Indian subcontinent: North Indian, South Indian, Tandoor and Nepali cuisine. Dine-in, to-go, delivery and catering options are available. We also provide buffet from 12-2 on weekdays and 11-3 on weekends.
